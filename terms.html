Natural stone (including marble, granite, quartzite, travertine, and similar materials) is a product of nature. Each slab is unique and may exhibit:

  • Variation in colour, tone, and pattern between slabs and within the same slab.

These natural variations are not considered defects and are not grounds for rejection, refund, or replacement. We will always aim to discuss slab selection and layout with you prior to fabrication where possible.
